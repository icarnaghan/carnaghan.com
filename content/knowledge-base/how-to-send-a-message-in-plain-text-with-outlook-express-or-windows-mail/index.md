title: "How to send a message in plain text with Outlook Express or Windows Mail"
date: 2018-04-06
---

- Open **Outlook Express** (**Start->All Programs->Outlook Express**)
    
    ![Start->All Programs->Outlook Express](images/oulooke1.png "Start->All Programs->Outlook Express")
- Once **Outlook Express** is open click on **Create Mail**![Create Mail](images/oulooke2.png "Create Mail")
- A new message will open up
    
    ![New Message](images/oulooke3.png "New Message")
- At the menu click on **Format->Plain Text** (To create a plain text message or to format the current mail to be plain text)
    
    ![Format->Plain Text](images/oulooke4.png "Format->Plain Text")
- A warning will popup just click on '**OK**'
    
    ![OK](images/oulooke5.png "OK")
- Now your message will be send using the **Plain Text format**

**NOTE**: To send mail permanently using plain text following the steps below

- Once in **Outlook Express** click on **Tools->Options
    
    **![Tools->Accounts](images/oulooke6.png "Tools->Accounts")
- Click on the '**Send**' tab and under **Mail Sending Format** select **Plain Text**![Send](images/oulooke7.png "Send")
- Click on '**OK**' to save your settings, now every new mail will be send using **Plain Text**
