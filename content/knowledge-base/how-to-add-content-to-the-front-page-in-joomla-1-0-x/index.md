title: "How to add content to the Front Page in Joomla! 1.0.x"
date: 2018-04-06
---

**STEP 1**

Click on **Content Items Manager** on the **Front Page** of the **Joomla! 1.0.x  interface**in the **Back End**

**STEP 2**

Once the **Content Items Manager** opens you'll see all of your **Content** in the List, under the heading **Front Page** you'll see a **Red Cross** for each **content article**.

**STEP 3**

Click on the **Red Cross** and it will be changed to a **Green Arrow or Checkmark.**

**STEP 4**

Once it has a **Green Checkmark**, it means that the **content** will now be **displayed** on your **Front Page**.

**STEP 5**

Switch back to the **Home Page on the Front End**, and now you'll see that each **Content Items Intro text** that was selected for the Front Page will now appear on the**Front Page** or **Home Page** of your **site**.
