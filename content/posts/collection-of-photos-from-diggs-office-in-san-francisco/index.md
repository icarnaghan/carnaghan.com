Digg is undoubtedly the most popular social news website on the internet. Hell, it sort of invented the social news genre itself! Digg has been a huge inspiration to me as a designer and developer, and I have learned so much through Digg's progression over the years. <!--more-->So I took it upon myself to find a bit more about the site: by digging around Flickr! I found a great collection of office photos inside Digg's main headquarters in San Francisco, CA, and I've posted it up here to share with all my readers.
